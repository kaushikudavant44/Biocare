Leadership


Bhavin Bharatia Founder & CEO

Bhavin Bharatia is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer at Bionische and carries with him exceptional industry acumen and a proven success record to manage and transform businesses to highly successful enterprises. Bhavin has previously served as a Middleware and LDAP administrator for IBM, he has also held the post of an Infrastructure Admin for Verizon, and SiteMinder Engineer for EY. Since 2009, he has been the CEO of WideArc Inc, where in he contributes his expertise for Talent Development, IT solutions, IT Staffing and Recruiting,& Healthcare. He has been a proven mentor and leader for many to achieve their goals, as well as a true business figure in providing technology driven business solutions and services.

His vision for better healthcare is a next generation platform wherein the complete process is digitized without any paperwork for well-timed care, optimum clinical management, enhanced information flow, consolidated data, and more. He aims to streamline all healthcare verticals, and create an ecosystem for patients, doctors, healthcare professionals, and users to stay healthful on the fly. Throughout his career, Bhavin has helped clients impact businesses through exceptional connections, relations, and support.
